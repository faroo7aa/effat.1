Now, let's ask the question: What is Heritage?

Heritage refers to the traditions, achievements, beliefs, and cultural artifacts that are passed down from previous generations. It includes elements like historical buildings, monuments, artworks, and artifacts, as well as languages, folklore, and practices. Heritage plays a key role in shaping the identity of a community, group, or nation and is preserved to maintain a connection to the past for future generations.

What is heritage preservation?

Heritage preservation refers to the practice of protecting, conserving, and maintaining cultural, historical, and natural heritage. The goal of heritage preservation is to safeguard these assets for future generations while promoting awareness and appreciation of their significance.
